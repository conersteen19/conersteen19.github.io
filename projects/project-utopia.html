Project Utopia

Senior Capstone ProjectWinter/Spring 2018

Project Utopia is a full-scale theme park designed in Planet Coaster.  The park was designed starting with the land features including creation of the river, hills, and forest.  It went on to include 5 unique, custom designed roller coasters with distinct features and styles.  The park also includes facilities and locations commonly found in parks such as stores, restaurants, and restrooms/guest facilities.

Coasters and the Design of Utopia

Project Utopia was really created around a love for Roller Coaster and Theme Park Design. Thus the rides were very specifically chosen to fit not only their surroundings but the area's theme and mimic their respective manufacturers. These manufacturers include a GCI wooden coaster with Millenium Flyers, Arrow Dynamics Looper, B&M Flying, Premier Sky Rocket, and B&M Wing coasters.

Note that Point of View videos of the coaster can be found on the YouTube Channel below.

Video Series

While developing the park, a virtual series was also created to show how and why my design decisions were made.  These videos were uploaded to YouTube to allow others to see the basic steps and hopefully inspire others to create their own parks.
