About Me

My name is Conner Steenrod.  I am a student at the University of Virginia studying Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science.

My passions are Amusement Parks, Mechatronics, Test Engineering, Mechanical and Structural Engineering, Network Engineering, and Cyber Security.  I have pursued all of these passions in different but unique ways and love to see how my different interests can work together to solve new problems.

I hope to have a career where I can use my skill sets to make change in the world, whether that comes from building testing aerospace engines, creating amusement park rides, designing components or structures, building networks, or working to secure machines and networks.
