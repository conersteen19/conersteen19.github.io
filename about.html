About Me

My name is Conner Steenrod.  I am a student at the University of Virginia studying Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science.

My passions are Amusement Parks, Mechanical and Structural Engineering, Network Engineering, Cyber Security, and Computer Science.  I have pursued all of these passions in different but unique ways and love to see how my different interests can work together to solve new problems.

I hope to have a career where I can use my skill sets to make change in the world, whether that comes from building amusement park rides, designing components or structures, building networks, or working to secure machines and networks.
